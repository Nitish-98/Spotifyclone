Frequently Asked Questions

How do I download songs?

+
Click on the "Download" option in the navigation bar to access the download page. From there, you can download any song by clicking the download button next to it.

How do I search for songs?

+
Use the search bar at the top of the page. Type the song name and the library will filter in real-time to show matching results.

What are the Premium benefits?

+
Premium includes ad-free listening, offline downloads, high-quality audio, and the ability to play any song on demand.

How do I control playback?

+
Use the playbar at the bottom of the screen. You can play/pause, skip to next/previous songs, and seek through the current track by clicking on the progress bar.

Can I create playlists?

+
Currently, you can browse and play songs from the library. Playlist creation features are coming soon!
